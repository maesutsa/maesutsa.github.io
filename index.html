About Us

We are the University of Texas at San Antonio student chapter of MAES, a student-managed organization that is designed to help students during their collegiate career and to ultimately get hired in the STEM field. MAES is the foremost Latino organization for the development of STEM leaders in the academic, executive, and technical communities. Our mission is to promote and expand as individuals and as an organization to create opportunities for all STEM minorities in San Antonio. Our student chapter has been one of the most successful and nationally recognized chapters in recent history. We are, to this day, the model student engineering organization and not only at the College of Engineering, but all throughout the UTSA campus.
