About MAES UTSA

We are the MAES student chapter for the University of Texas at San Antonio, a student-managed organization that is designed to help students during their collegiate career and to ultimately get hired in the STEM field.

Our chapter has been one of the most successful and nationally recognized chapters in recent history due to our outreach, volunteer work, leadership, and dedication.

We are, to this day, the model student engineering organization and not only at the College of Engineering, but all throughout the UTSA campus.

Join MAES

Joining us can impact your career and student experience in a positive way. You will gain skills, expand your resume, make connections, and most importantly you will have fun!

To become a full MAES member, all you have to do is attend our general meetings which are held on campus and pay yearly dues of $10.

Meetings help to inform about all the events such as career opportunities, social events, fundraisers, intramural sports, and company tours. We also share information about our events on Slack and Calendar.

Paying dues comes with all sorts of benefits like being able to attend special events, receive MAES shirts, and allows us to do cool stuff for MAES members. Once you pay your dues at the next meeting, you will be given a membership coupon code which you will use to activate by clicking the box below.
